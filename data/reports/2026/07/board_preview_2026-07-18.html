Sensor Check - Predicted vs Actual

BOM averages predicted 9.7 mm of lake losses over the rolling 7 days to this morning; after counting 10.0 mm of rain, the lake sensor measured 7.2 mm of actual net losses (evaporation, seepage and inflows combined) - 26% below the seasonal average. This alone moved the projected cease date about 0.4 days later. (Endpoints use 24 h smoothing windows, so they differ slightly from the daily table.) The projection keeps using long-term BOM averages (stable planning basis) and restarts from the measured lake level each Monday, so real weather corrects it automatically.
