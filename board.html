Season Water Saving vs Town Water Supply
--
Estimated saving since season start
Town-supplied irrigation while ceased: $0 - irrigation switches to tank (town) water only when the lake reaches the cease-to-pump level.
Off-season period: Irrigation ends mid-May and resumes in September - no irrigation runs from the lake through winter, so savings are $0 for these months. Savings accumulate from September. Data tracking commenced 20 May 2026.
